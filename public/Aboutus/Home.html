About Dishtreet

Dishtreet is an e-food ordering system for street vendors.The vision is to help street vendors take their business online from where people can catch sight of mouse watering dishes and order them online.The platform helps vendors to showcase their dishes online.People can simply check various available street vendors and dishes they provide and get their food delivered.
